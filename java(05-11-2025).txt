finally block:

-finally block is used, to execute the statements or instructions before JVM shutsDown, under normal or abnormal condition.

-Where do we use final blocks in real time?
->We use finally blocks: to destroy objects in the memory, to close database connectivity, to close streams .

- the throes keyword is responsible to delegate(pospone) the exception but cant handle the exception.
- the try-catch block can only handle the exception. An exception can be raised either during compilation or runtime, but it can be handled only during runtime.


-only checked exceptions are delegated, unchecked exceptions are not delegated.(tire puncture example)
