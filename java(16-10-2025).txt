ENCAPSULATION:

-Encapsulation is a process of binding a members together, to ensure it is protected; this process is called as encapsulation.

-In a normal encapsulation, there are chances that other classes may be able to access the data members by object creation or classname, this will involve manupilation of other class data members, and this process of encapsulation, may not be secured, hence we go for tightly bound encapsulation.---->(private access modifier use karto)

-By making the data members private , we can achieve tightly bound encapsulation.
